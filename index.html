About

Guillermo Murúa is a self-studied web developer and freelancer mostly active in the brazillian startup community.

Usually he takes the spot as frontend developer, but he's no stranger to other such eandeavors as building features front-to-back, TDD and service development/integration. He specially likes to work with a Javascript toolset.

On his free time, Guillermo engages in self learning learning math and languages, writing/blogging, toying with experimental projects and, apperantly, being turned into an artpiece by his colleagues.
